Everything about Google reviews for bed & breakfasts

Digital word of mouth: Google reviews for guesthouses

B&Bs used to fill their rooms thanks to returning guests and recommendations passed along the road. Today that recommendation has gone digital. When a traveller searches for accommodation in Dingle, Kenmare or Doolin, the first results are the guesthouses in the local pack with their star ratings. A B&B with 4.8 stars and 60 reviews will be booked ahead of a lovely house down the lane that only has 3 reviews.

The tourist season: when every star counts

From spring right through to the end of the season, visitors touring the Ring of Kerry and the Wild Atlantic Way book their nights on the go, often the same day. They have no time to compare a dozen listings, so they choose the guesthouse with the best rating that shows up nearby on Google Maps and Booking. Your Google reviews are your best host, welcoming guests 24 hours a day.

Own your area thanks to reviews

A bed & breakfast is a hyper-local business. Google ranks accommodation by proximity and rating. With enough Google reviews for your guesthouse, you appear at the top of the results in your town and along the nearby tourist trail. It's an investment that translates directly into fuller rooms and a longer season.
